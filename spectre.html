Project Name: Spectre Literary
What I Did: Web Design, Development
Live site: Coming soon!



Everyone has a story to tell

Spectre Literary is a freelance project I took on for an independent ghostwriting company. The vision of Spectre was to make ghostwriting services easily accessible.

Spectre commissioned me to create a totally new website for their service with the idea that it should feel, "Professional, high-class, and modern." I moved forward with these ideals to create three different directions for the website. The idea that won uses a half and half design to represent the layout of a book. This unusual layout also serves to make the page feel more interesting and unique. The black and white color scheme keeps things high class, but modern at the same time. I used a serif font to bring in some classic literary charm.

It was an exciting challenge to find a happy medium between classic and modern. The client wanted the literary nature of the site to shine through, but not to feel too old-fashioned. Overall, what we settled on was a clean and modern experience for a user who wants to tell their story to the world.
