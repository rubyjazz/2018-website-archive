What will you build next?

This project tasked me with creating packaging for a limited time vinyl to coincide with GitHub's What Will You Build Next? campaign. The campaign revolved around celebrating the work that people have done with GitHub over the past 10 years and looking forward to the next projects people will create.

To get this idea across, I took used a transparent slipcover on top of a plain white box. This slipcover is covered in clothes, paint, and bright shapes which create an environment for the vinyl. The slip cover conveys the idea that this is something you should draw on and gives inspiration for decorating your figurine. Once the slip is removed, the plain white box is revealed giving a sense of excitement of creation. The idea is that the box should reflect the meaning of the figurine without needing to read anything.

It's the feeling of creativity and possibility for the future that makes this a good tie in to the campaign, and hopefully an inspiration to create new projects in the future!
