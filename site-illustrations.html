Location: London



Satellite 2017

Satellite is an inclusive tech conference for the modern age.

I focused on discovery, expession, and coming together for this London conference. Satellite is a yearly GitHub conference that promotes the advances in the tech community and brings developers together for a few days of inspiration and general geeking out. For this project, Satellite needed a new brand. The conference falls in the ecosystem of other GitHub conferences, but the style changes every year. For this year I chose to take inspiration from space and spirit of exploration. I sampled colors from nebulas and galaxies and put them within the confines of an organic shape to show that the important thing is what's inbetween. Just like a satellite circles it's parent planet, this conference circles GitHub and brings people together.
